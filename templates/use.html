Speech Emotion Recognition

Speech Emotion Recognition (SER) is the task of recognizing the emotion from speech irrespective of the semantic contents. However, emotions are subjective and even for humans it is hard to notate them in natural speech communication regardless of the meaning. Let see the demonstration of How to Use this project to recognize the emotions from the audio.

How to Use?

Step 1: Click on Get Started.

Step 2: Choose the existing file or record a live audio to recognize the emotion.

Step 3: After selecting the audio click on recognize.

Step 4: Now you can see the MFCCS result to view the Mel-spectrum result click on Mel-Spectrum.

Step 5: To check the recent activity click on the icon of Recent Activity.

Step 6: To view About Us page click on about us icon.

Step 7: To navigate back to the Home Page click on Home icon.
